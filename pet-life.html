A Sneak Peak Into The World of Pets

Being a pet is certainly not a walk in the park neither is it a hell,far from it. It is just life like that of the human being with its own ups and downs. Would I as a pet want to opt out? Well, you can judge for yourself!

The worst part about being a pet is:

  • Having to be caddled even when I am not in the mood
  • Living at the mercies of humans
  • Most caregivers don't understand my language,they just assume they do but they don't
  • Arranged marriages
  • Forced to be family with those who are not my kind,while my real family(children) is snatched from me
